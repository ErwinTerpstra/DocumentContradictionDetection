New York State Route 31B was a state highway in central New York in the United States.
New York State Route 31B served as a connector between New York State Route 31 in the Cayuga County village of Weedsport and New York State Route 5 in the Onondaga County town of Elbridge.
New York State Route 31B was assigned around 1933.
New York State Route 31B replaced New York State Route 293.
New York State Route 293 was a route assigned as part of the 1930 renumbering of state highways in New York.
The New York State Route 31B designation was removed in 1980.
County Route 31B replaced New York State Route 31B in Cayuga County.
County Route 99 replaced New York State Route 31B in Onondaga County.
New York State Route 31B began at an intersection with New York State Route 31 in the Cayuga County village of Weedsport.
New York State Route 31B went eastward, intersecting with New York State Route 34 less than 0.1 miles (0.2 km) later.
Much of Weedsport was urbanized.
New York State Route 31B passed residential homes and businesses as it progressed eastward through the village of Weedsport.
New York State Route 31B intersected with County Route 12B before leaving Weedsport.
New York State Route 31B entered the town of Brutus as Brutus Road.
In Brutus, New York State Route 31B continued to the east through the rural town.
New York State Route 31B intersected County Route 136A in Brutus.
New York State Route 31B passed the Weedsport Rural Cemetery before turning to the southeast.
New York State Route 31B went through an isolated area of homes.
New York State Route 31B intersected with County Route 14 in the isolated area of homes.
New York State Route 31B intersected with County Route 15A in the isolated area of homes.
New York State Route 31B continued on, intersecting several local highways.
New York State Route 31B crossed into Onondaga County.

Ownership and maintenance of New York State Route 31B's former routing was gradually transferred to the counties it went through.
